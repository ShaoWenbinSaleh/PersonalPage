Y3ComicCon

A native iOS application customised for Y3 ComicCon

An outsourcing project all done by myself in one week. This is a small application customized for one advertisement company to publish news about the comic-con it organizes. Simple services about selling tickets and navigation are also supplied. The front-end is developed by purely objective-c (the MVB pattern). Its back-end is developed by a cloud service in China called LeanCloud. Therefore, only after updating the database on the cloud, newest news and comic-con information can be displayed on the phone in the most effective way.
